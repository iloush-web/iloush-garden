Facts


  • This is the first camera I've ever bought!
    I got it second-hand from a girl who was selling it because she didn't need it anymore — she said her phone covers all her photo needs. I was so happy:
    the camera is in excellent condition and came with a full set, even the original box.
  • I've had this camera for a long time.
    It's upsetting that it's starting to wear out — some buttons don't respond on the first press, and the "Help menu" button now randomly starts video recording instead of opening the "help menu". I'm not sure what caused it, maybe years of active use.
  • I've shot so many videos with this camera.
    The resolution is really great, and the autofocus is slow — but I'm used to it.
  • This camera has NFC and Wi-Fi
    , which is really convenient. You can transfer photos to your phone or even control the camera remotely. The remote mode only works in automatic settings, but it's still handy.
