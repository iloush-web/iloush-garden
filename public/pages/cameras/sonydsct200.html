    f/3.5—4.4; 5.8—29 mm
  • Optical zoom:
    5x

    1/2.5" Super HAD CCD™

    8.1 MP

    3.5" CCD, touch screen
  • Flash:
    Built-in

    JPEG, MPEG-1
  • Video resolution:
    MPEG VX Fine, 640*480, 30 fps
  • Storage media:
    Memory Stick Duo, Memory Stick PRO Duo

Facts


  • I bought this camera online
    , without the ability to check if it was working. At the post office, I could only inspect its appearance -
    I wasn't allowed to turn it on
    (kind of strange rules). But I compared the serial number on the camera with the photos and videos from the seller, and it matched.
    Seller was really nice — he even gave me a cute camera bag. And the camera came with almost a full set of accessories.
  • These cameras come with a stylus,
    and I was specifically looking for one that included it (it was really hard to find). Now I can draw on photos!!!!!! (so worth it).
  • So many photo editing features!
    I can draw, add stamps, apply frames, sparkles, and color effects.
